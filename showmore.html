eHospital Objectives

  • To provide the eHospital, e-BloodBank, and ORS applications to government hospitals.
  • To provide an online patient portal for citizen-centric services like online appointment booking, access to lab reports, and blood availability status.
  • To provide application-related technical support to hospitals through a dedicated Call Centre/Helpdesk.

About Us

Welcome to LifeCare eHospital!

Under the Digital India initiative of the Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology, Government of India, National Informatics Centre (NIC) has developed the eHospital, e-BloodBank and ORS (Online Registration System) applications.

The eHospital application is the Hospital Management Information System (HMIS) for internal workflows and processes of hospitals. eHospital is a one-stop solution which helps in connecting patients, hospitals and doctors on a single digital platform.

eHospital Modules

Patient Registration (OPD, Casualty, Appointment & ORS)

The patient registration module of the eHospital application is used for patient registration in the OPD and Casualty departments as well as to book, confirm and cancel appointments.

Admission, Discharge & Transfer (IPD)

The IPD module commences when the patient is being registered and allotted a bed in the ward. It deals with the complete treatment and services provided to the patient during their stay in the hospital.

Billing

The Billing module handles all types of billing workflows. This module facilitates cashier and billing operators for managing billing functions related to billing receipts and refunds.

Clinic (OPD & IPD)

The Clinic module allows the clinicians and doctors to record the clinical data of the patients like visits, examination, diagnosis, history, treatment, prescriptions, investigations, and procedures.

Lab Information System (LIS)

Automates laboratory tasks such as ordering tests, analyzing results, and managing lab workflows.

Radiology Information System (RIS)

Manages radiology workflows, scheduling tests, verifying results, and providing clinical treatment.

Store & Pharmacy

Manages medical stores, inventory, and supplies across the hospital departments.

OT Management

Automates workflows and functions of hospital operation theatres.

Dietary

Manages dietary services provided to patients.

Ambulance

Automates ambulance management and patient transportation.
